Lift The Ball

Video Demo

Objectives

To lift the three (3) balls with the same color out of the cylindrical chicken wire.

Materials

  • Cylindrical chicken wire
  • Barbeque sticks
  • Assorted plastic balls

Mechanics

  • This activity can be played either outdoor or indoor.
  • There must be two (2) opposing teams with equal number of players.
  • Each team will have one cylindrical chicken wire with balls inside and one stick per member.
  • Players must work together to lift the three balls one at a time out of the wire.
  • Transfer the balls from starting point to the basket on the opposite side.
  • The team with the shortest time wins.

Rules

  • Balls must be transferred using the sticks only.
  • If a ball falls, the team must restart from the starting point.

Values

This activity enhances coordination and decision-making as participants work together as one team to achieve the goal.
