Stack It, Balance It

Video Demo

Objectives

To develop trust, focus, and precision while performing coordinated team tasks under pressure.

Materials

  • Cups
  • Ball
  • Basket

Mechanics

  • The group must consist of five to ten players.
  • The group will form a straight line.
  • The player should build the cups in pyramid stacks (4, 3, 2, 1).
  • After forming, the ball must be placed on top and then unstacked without falling.
  • Players are allowed to use both hands to stack the cups.

Rules

  • Only one hand may be used when unstacking the cups.
  • If the ball falls, the team must start over.
  • The next player starts once the previous finishes retrieving the stack.
  • The group who finishes first wins.
  • For races, the group with the shortest time wins.

Values

Slowly but surely. It is better to take it slowly rather than rush without assurance of a good result.
